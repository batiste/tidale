DeckHand — Race for Infamy

  • 2-5 players
  • 30-45 minutes
  • Simultaneous play
  • Shared objectives

Players draft from 96 uniquely illustrated pirate-themed cards, each offering distinct actions and effects.

Trade rum, guns, and tame wild creatures to build your fleet, balancing resources and strategy to outmaneuver opponents. Seek powerful Infamy cards, or block your rivals from getting what they need.

Will you tap into the dark allure of the occult to gain an edge, explore emerald jungles where adventure and corruption offer wealth, or master trade and tactics to secure supremacy on the high seas?

  • Age: 14+
  • Tableau building
  • Moderate interaction
  • Draft manipulation

With each card built, players enhance their tableau, unlocking new opportunities and expanding their influence.
Competing for limited Infamy cards heightens the tension, forcing you to anticipate your opponents’ moves and adapt your strategies.

  • Medium complexity
  • Resource tree
  • Set collection
  • 108 Poker cards
Try the game on Screentop.gg How to Play

If you enjoyed 7 Wonders, Race for the Galaxy, Splendor, or It’s a Wonderful World, this game brings a fresh mix of those mechanics with added layers of interaction, planning, and challenging decisions.
