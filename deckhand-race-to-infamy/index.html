As an aspiring pirate, you will draft and build your fleet to expand your influence and compete for coveted Infamy cards.

Play with 108 uniquely illustrated pirate-themed cards to trade rum, guns, and tame wild creatures. Build your resource engine, outmaneuver rivals, and pivot strategy to claim victory.

Will you succumb to the allure of the occult, venture into the jungle in search of wealth, or dominate the trade routes?

  • Simultaneous actions
  • Shared objectives
  • Engine building

Each card you build strengthens your fleet, unlocking new opportunities and expanding your reach for Infamy.

Simultaneous play opens the door to bluffs and strategic positioning. Will you focus on building your resource engine or racing for Infamy cards?
