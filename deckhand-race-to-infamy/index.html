Players draft from 84 uniquely illustrated, pirate-themed cards to trade rum, guns, and tame wild creatures. Build your fleet while balancing resources and strategy to outmaneuver your rivals. Seek powerful Infamy cards or thwart your opponents' plans at every turn.

Will you embrace the dark allure of the occult to gain an edge? Venture into emerald jungles in search of wealth? Or master trade and tactics to dominate the high seas?

  • Age: 14+
  • Tableau building
  • Moderate interaction
  • Draft manipulation

With each card built, players enhance their fleet, unlocking new opportunities and expanding their influence.

Competing for limited Infamy cards heightens the tension, forcing you to anticipate your opponents’ moves and adapt your strategies.

  • Medium complexity
  • Resource tree
  • Set collection
  • 108 Poker cards

The game is free to play with up to 4 players on Screentop.gg. Gather some friends, give it a try, and see if it’s your cup of tea. If you enjoy it, consider backing the project on Kickstarter to get your own physical copy!

If you enjoyed 7 Wonders, Race for the Galaxy, Splendor, or It’s a Wonderful World, this game brings a fresh mix of those mechanics with added layers of interaction, planning, and challenging decisions.
