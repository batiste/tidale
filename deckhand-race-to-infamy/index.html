DeckHand — Race for Infamy

A battle of wits in a vibrant world of adventure, for up to 5 players.

  • Simple rules & setup
  • Tableau building
  • Set collection
  • Simultaneous play
  • Shared objectives
  • Resource tree
  • Light interaction
  • Draft manipulation

Each of your decision could lead to fame or defeat. Over six rounds, players will strategically draft from a pool of 96 carefully illustrated cards, each featuring unique pirate-themed actions and effects.

As you trade rum, guns, and tame wild creatures, you'll build your fleet, balancing resource management with the need to outmaneuver your opponents. Aim to claim powerful Infamy cards that can influence the game, or deny your opponent what they need, making every round a test of strategy, timing, and bold choices.

As the game unfolds, players engage in simultaneous actions. With each card built, players enhance their tableau and unlock opportunities for scoring Infamy points at the end of the game. Competing for limited Infamy cards adds tension, requiring you to anticipate your opponents’ moves and adapt your strategies. Will you become the most infamous pirate, or will you be left behind?
