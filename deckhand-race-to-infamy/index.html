Each of your decisions could lead to fame or defeat. Over six rounds, players will strategically draft from a pool of 96 carefully illustrated cards, each featuring unique pirate-themed actions and effects.

As you trade rum, guns, and tame wild creatures, you'll build your fleet, balancing resource management with the need to outmaneuver your opponents. Aim to claim powerful Infamy cards that can influence the game, or deny your opponent what they need, making every round a test of strategy, timing, and bold choices.

Embrace the dark allure of the occult to gain an edge over your rivals. Venture into the emerald jungles, where adventure and corruption present opportunities for wealth. Master the art of trade and tactics to achieve navigation and commerce supremacy.

With each card built, players enhance their tableau, unlocking new opportunities, expanding their influence, and gaining recognition for their exploits. Competing for limited Infamy cards heightens the tension, forcing you to anticipate your opponents’ moves and adapt your strategies. Will you rise to become the most infamous pirate, or will you be left behind in the quest for glory?
